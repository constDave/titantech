The Titan Lab Tech Hamer Screens filtration system is the most advanced and cost-effective method for separating solids from effluent and wastewater. The water is channeled through the screen at a channeled back to the target destination. The system is capable of filtering solids from 25 – 5000 consistent flow rate and the separated solids will wash down the screen while the treated water is microns and can be configured to allow for high or low flow rates.

Benefits:

  • Removes solids between 25 – 5000 microns.
  • Small and compact footprint
  • Low operating energy cost
  • Easily scalable to adjust for higher or lower flow rates
  • Custom sizing available
  • Major savings on water bills

The Process

Did you know that solids in your Process liquid or Wastewater have a direct affect on your discharge quality that can affect your downstream process ? Sand or grit can clog pipes and a conventional screen. Solids in your wastewater have a direct link to your BOD & COD.
